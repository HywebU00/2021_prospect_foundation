    Prospect Quarterly (Chinese)

    The Prospect Quarterly (ISSN 1560-1696), a brainchild of the Prospect Foundation, is an academic journal, with a special focus on International Relations, mainland China Issues, Asia-Pacific Issues, cross-Strait Relations, and Regional Security. The Prospect Quarterly is published on January, April, July, and October.

    Taiwan Strategists

    The Taiwan Strategists (ISSN 2663-6174), a brainchild of the Prospect Foundation, is published to facilitate exchanges with think tanks and academia globally. The Journal is a forum for expounding the views of renowned Taiwanese scholars on International Affairs, with a special focus on Asia-Pacific Issues, Regional Security, the Development of mainland China, cross-Strait Relations, and other related topics. The Taiwan Strategists is published on March, June, September, and December.
